    About Us

    Who are we?

    Ocean Biome is a community of talents with a focus on ocean research to map the path to engage in Ocean Protection to enable the next generation of leaders to engage in ocean literacy and stewardship to become ocean ambassadors in Sri Lanka.

    What is our mission

    Our mission of the OceanBiome organization is to welcome all the people into the field of ocean and marine life. Educating, empathising and awaring everyone to work for a common motive. Preserving the marine resources and saving the endangered things to build up a sustainable marine ecosystem.
